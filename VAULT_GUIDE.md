## Intentionally Separate

- `.agents/`, `.claude/`, `.codex/` are provider/runtime surfaces, not part of
  the main project knowledge graph.
- `knowledge.db`, `story.db`, screenshots, and JSON/runtime artifacts are
  operational files, not note nodes.
- Generated scene and canon docs are grouped under their own output indexes so
  they do not overwhelm the engineering graph.
